# Code of Conduct

vouch adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1][cc] as its code of conduct.

## In short

- Be civil. Disagree with ideas, not people.
- No harassment, slurs, doxing, or targeted hostility.
- Assume the other person is acting in good faith until proven otherwise.
- If something feels off, raise it privately first.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues / PRs / discussions, any
official chat, and conduct in other venues when representing the project.

## Reporting

Email **conduct@vouch.invalid** (placeholder until a real address is
available; until then, open a confidential GitHub security advisory and tag
it `conduct:`).

Reports are read by current maintainers. We aim to acknowledge within
72 hours and to act — warn, mediate, suspend, or ban — proportionate to
the situation. Reports are kept confidential to the extent practical.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues / PRs, and revoke access from
contributors who breach this code. Repeated or severe breaches lead to a
permanent ban. Decisions are made by simple maintainer majority; the
reporting maintainer recuses if involved.

## Full text

The full Contributor Covenant 2.1 text governs anything not addressed
above: [https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/][cc].
